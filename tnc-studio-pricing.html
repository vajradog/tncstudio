Number of Panoramas?

A single 360* panorama is an unbroken view of the whole region surrounding an observer.

A virtual tour is made up of several such panorama shots, following a set of rules; each panoramas are spaced to provide the best virtual tour of the venue. Number of panoramas will depend on how much your venue is.
